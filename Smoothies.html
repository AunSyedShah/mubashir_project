Smoothies

Sip Fresh Stay Energized
Tropical Mango Coconut Smoothie

1 cup frozen mango chunks 1/2 cup coconut milk 1/2 cup pineapple juice 1 tablespoon shredded coconut (optional) 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (optional) Ice cubes

Green Detox Smoothie

1 cup spinach 1/2 cucumber 1/2 green apple 1/2 lemon (juice) 1 tablespoon chia seeds 1 cup coconut water or water Ice cubes

Berry Blast Smoothie

1/2 cup strawberries 1/2 cup blueberries 1/2 cup raspberries 1/2 cup almond milk 1 tablespoon honey or agave 1/2 banana

Strawberry Kiwi Smoothie

1/2 cup frozen strawberries 1 ripe kiwi 1/2 banana 1/2 cup Greek yogurt 1 tablespoon honey Ice cubes

Pineapple & Ginger Smoothie

1 cup fresh pineapple chunks 1/2-inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled 1/2 cup coconut water 1/2 banana 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Cucumber Mint Smoothie

1 cucumber, peeled 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves 1/2 cup Greek yogurt or coconut yogurt 1 tablespoon honey 1/2 cup water Ice cubes

Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

1 banana 1 tablespoon peanut butter 1 cup milk (or almond milk) 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup Ice cubes

Chocolate Protein Smoothie

1 scoop chocolate protein powder 1/2 banana 1 tablespoon almond butter 1 cup milk (or oat milk) 1 tablespoon cocoa powder Ice cubes

Avocado & Mango Smoothie

1/2 avocado 1/2 cup frozen mango 1/2 cup orange juice 1/2 cup water or almond milk 1 tablespoon honey or sweetener of choice
